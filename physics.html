About the Physics Course

This course explores the fundamental principles of physics including mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, and modern physics. It is tailored to provide a strong conceptual foundation and problem-solving skills for students at all levels.

Duration: 14 weeks

Instructor: Dr. Brian Newton, PhD in Theoretical Physics

Goals:

By the end of the course, students will have a deep understanding of physical laws and the ability to apply them in real-world and theoretical scenarios.
